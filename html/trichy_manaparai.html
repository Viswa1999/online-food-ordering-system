The Delicious Treat of Trichy Famous Manapaarai Murukku

19 FEB 2021

Muruku is a renowned and a routine snack, it is basically crispy swirls made with standard rice flour or arisi maavu. Many traditional ingredients are also added, like cumin seeds, omam, hing, and ghee to give the best and tastiest murukku just for your lovable family and friends.

Manapparai Murukku is an impressive variety of murukku from Manapparai in the Trichy District in Tamil Nadu state.â€¦

What is the Special Here?

This crunchy murukku business started by Mr. Mani Iyer, who sold this in the railway station a few years back. Now it is one of the famous murukkus in Tamil Nadu, India.

Manapparai is a well-known town which is famous for the traditional rice or Arisi Murukku.!!!

-Mr.Mani Iyer

There is a vast difference between Manapparai Murukku vs. normal murukku because of the ingredients used, particularly ghee, that is deep-fried to perfection.Enjoy this super crunchy snack with a cup of tea, coffee, or with your daily meal that tastes delicious.
